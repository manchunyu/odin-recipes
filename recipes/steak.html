My Perfect Medium-Rare Steak

Description

The dish that got me a girlfriend!!

My girlfriend was so impressed after having My Perfect Medium-Rare Steak, that she agreed to date me. And I am going to show you how to make it now. So you can impress the girl of your dream!

Ingredients

  • Aberdeen Angus Sirloin Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Let it marinate while you prepare the other ingredients.
  3. Peel the skin off the garlic, no need to slice or dice it.
  4. Heat up your pan using high heat.
  5. Once the pan is burning hot, add olive oil and turn to low heat.
  6. Lay the steak on the pan and sear it for 1 minutes each side.
  7. Add garlic and thyme after 1 minute on each side, and add butter.
  8. After 3 minutes, turn off the gas, and base the steak for 1 minute on each side.
  9. Serve!
